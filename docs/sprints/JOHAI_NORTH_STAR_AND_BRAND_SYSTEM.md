## Objective

Create the permanent strategic foundation that guides JOHAI's employees, investors, designers, developers, product leaders, sales teams, AI Employees, and customer experiences.

## Problem solved

The Constitution defined the highest internal product-design laws, but JOHAI also needed one durable account of why the company exists, what success means, how the company behaves, how its brand communicates, and how long-term ambition must be evaluated without turning aspiration into a current capability claim.

- Makes trust, useful intelligence, human relationships, and reduced operational work enduring strategic constraints.
- Gives future teams observable standards for language, visual direction, AI Employee behavior, culture, and roadmap decisions.
- Reduces strategic drift and contradictory audience claims.

## Architecture changes

Documentation governance now requires every future architectural or product decision to align with the JOHAI Constitution, North Star, Manifesto, AI Employee Principles, Product Promise, and Company Values.

The authority order is:

1. Applicable law, contracts, security, privacy, accessibility, explicit human approval, and mandatory database safety.
2. The JOHAI Constitution as the highest internal product-design authority.
3. The constitutional companion documents.
4. The strategic and brand foundation, interpreted consistently with those companion documents.
5. Approved architecture, policies, manuals, specifications, and implementation records.

The North Star defines enduring direction; the Manifesto explains conviction; Mission and Vision define purpose and destination; the Product Promise defines the experience owed; Company Values and Culture govern conduct; Brand and Voice govern expression; AI Employee Principles govern role behavior; and the roadmap describes directional horizons.

## Constitutional review

- **Owning layer:** Cross-platform company and product governance; not a new platform layer.
- **Laws affected:** All Ten Laws, especially reducing effort, justified confidence, natural human takeover, and the business as hero.
- **Values affected:** Trust, Simplicity, Speed, Transparency, Respect, Reliability, Privacy, Context, Elegance, and Business Value.
- **Decision-framework result:** Proceed to scoped design for documentation architecture. It clarifies purpose, trust, behavior, and long-term direction without claiming implementation.
- **Human authority:** Strategic adoption remains a human governance decision. AI cannot amend this foundation or approve departures from it.

## Known limitations and remaining risks
