# Investor Roadmap

Near-term milestones are tenant security, billing persistence, production retrieval, job reliability, automated tests, monitoring, and customer validation. Later expansion may add messaging, voice, and workplace channels.

## Status discipline

See [Product Fact Sheet](../PRODUCT_FACT_SHEET.md) for Implemented, Partial, and Planned classifications. Update this chapter when the corresponding product behavior changes.

## Constitutional gate

Every milestone remains subject to the [JOHAI Constitution](../constitution/JOHAI_CONSTITUTION.md). Roadmap placement does not authorize a feature or prove delivery; each proposal must reduce effort, create trust, remove friction, respect context, preserve human authority, demonstrate business value, and pass its security, accessibility, operational, and evidence gates.

## Long-term strategic horizon

The [Long-Term Roadmap](../foundation/LONG_TERM_ROADMAP.md) describes 1-, 3-, 5-, and 10-year strategic horizons across the platform, AI, marketplace, developer ecosystem, businesses, customers, and responsible international expansion. It is governed by the [North Star](../foundation/JOHAI_NORTH_STAR.md), [Mission and Vision](../foundation/MISSION_AND_VISION.md), and [Company Values](../foundation/COMPANY_VALUES.md).

These horizons are directional sequencing, not delivery commitments, financial forecasts, operating guidance, or proof of current capability. Each milestone retains its own funding, evidence, security, legal, accessibility, operational, and human-approval gates.

## Contextual Intelligence™ roadmap

The design philosophy is adopted; implementation remains staged and **Planned**. Near-term work defines permissioned context contracts, provenance and freshness, deterministic fallbacks, and measurement. Subsequent pilots add read-only suggestions inside existing document, message, appointment, profile, and support workflows, followed by verified human takeover and one industry pattern at a time.

Payments, orders, reservations, invoices, regulated explanations, and proactive recommendations require their own product, security, compliance, and consent contracts. No Contextual Intelligence milestone becomes an investor or production claim until it has implementation evidence, tenant-isolation and safety results, measured effort reduction, and customer outcome data.

## Customer Portal gate

The database foundation is Applied and Verified by explicit user confirmation, and the repository application is implemented. The next milestones are trusted customer provisioning and invitation redemption, production configuration and deployment, rate limiting, direct-REST/effective-privilege review, end-to-end cross-tenant and storage validation, monitored pilots, and measured customer value. No production or commercial milestone is claimed complete.
