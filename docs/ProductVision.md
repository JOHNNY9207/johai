# Product Vision

## Purpose
JOHAI is an AI employee platform for subscribed businesses. It helps owners understand what happened, what matters, what requires attention, and what JOHAI recommends.

## Architecture
The product centers on the Command Center. Supporting workspaces include CRM, AI Brain, Automations, Analytics, and Settings. Each workspace is prepared for multi-tenant business ownership.

## Components
Command Center, Morning Brief, AI Chief of Staff, CRM, Business Brain, Knowledge Engine, Semantic Memory, AI Orchestrator, Audit Engine, Automations, and Settings.

## Current Status
JOHAI supports an internal default business, lead capture, CRM management, dashboard authentication, Calendly, email notifications, follow-ups, onboarding, knowledge management, AI Brain preparation, executive insights, and a premium dashboard redesign.

## Future Roadmap
Business self-serve signup, full business user authentication, billing, WhatsApp/SMS/voice/Slack/Teams integrations, real semantic search, and tenant-specific AI assistants.

## Dependencies
Supabase multi-tenant data, Next.js dashboard, AI services, Calendly, and email providers.

## Known Limitations
Full SaaS account ownership is prepared but not complete, and some recommendations are deterministic until deeper AI providers are connected.
