# Calendly and Booking

JOHAI supports public booking configuration, availability lookup, settings, signed webhooks, and lead updates. Valid Calendly credentials and webhook configuration are required.

## Status discipline

See [Product Fact Sheet](../PRODUCT_FACT_SHEET.md) for Implemented, Partial, and Planned classifications. Update this chapter when the corresponding product behavior changes.

## Customer Portal appointments

Provisioned portal customers can review RLS-visible appointments, status, time, location, customer-visible notes, and safe stored HTTPS links for meeting, rescheduling, or cancellation. Those links open external provider destinations; JOHAI does not guarantee provider availability or synchronize changes from the portal itself. The portal does not expose lead records, CRM notes, or provider credentials.
