# Glossary

**AI employee:** product framing for bounded AI-assisted work. **Business Brain:** structured assessment of business knowledge. **Orchestration:** planning and recording permitted AI actions. **Recorded:** stored source data. **Derived:** calculated interpretation. **Estimated:** non-authoritative projection. **Recommended:** suggested human action.

**Customer Portal:** the separate `/portal/*` product layer for a provisioned end customer; it is not the Business Workspace. **Customer profile:** the RLS-visible identity link between one authenticated user and one JOHAI business. **Portal tenant context:** the selected customer-profile/business pair repeated on portal data operations; the selection is not authority without RLS. **Shared document:** current non-revoked document metadata explicitly scoped to one customer and business. **Document acknowledgement:** the customer's recorded acknowledgement of a shared document; it is not by itself immutable audit proof.
