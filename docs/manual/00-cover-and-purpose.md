# JOHAI Manual

The living product manual for JOHAI, an AI employee and executive operating system. This manual describes verified behavior, partial systems, operating limits, and future direction.

## Governing authority

The [JOHAI Constitution](../constitution/JOHAI_CONSTITUTION.md) is the highest JOHAI product-design authority. Every chapter, feature, interface, AI behavior, workflow, automation, and interaction is interpreted through its Ten Laws, product values, and decision framework. [Contextual Intelligence](../philosophy/CONTEXTUAL_INTELLIGENCE.md) is a subordinate customer-interaction doctrine.

When this manual conflicts with the Constitution, implementation stops and the conflict must be resolved explicitly. Constitutional adoption does not change runtime behavior or the status of any capability.

## Strategic foundation

The [JOHAI North Star](../foundation/JOHAI_NORTH_STAR.md), [Manifesto](../foundation/JOHAI_MANIFESTO.md), [Mission and Vision](../foundation/MISSION_AND_VISION.md), [Product Promise](../foundation/PRODUCT_PROMISE.md), and [Company Values](../foundation/COMPANY_VALUES.md) define the durable purpose and standards this manual serves. The [Brand Guidelines](../foundation/BRAND_GUIDELINES.md), [Voice and Tone](../foundation/VOICE_AND_TONE.md), [AI Employee Principles](../foundation/AI_EMPLOYEE_PRINCIPLES.md), [Culture](../foundation/CULTURE.md), and [Long-Term Roadmap](../foundation/LONG_TERM_ROADMAP.md) translate that identity into audience, operating, and horizon guidance.

These foundation documents guide decisions beneath the Constitution. They do not override law or protective controls, authorize implementation, or change a capability's evidence-based status. Contextual Intelligence remains a subordinate doctrine wherever contextual behavior is involved.
