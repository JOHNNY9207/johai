# Product Positioning

Position JOHAI as an AI Chief of Staff for service businesses: it connects customer response, business knowledge, lead operations, booking, and executive decisions. Do not promise unrestricted autonomy.

## Constitutional positioning

The [JOHAI Constitution](../constitution/JOHAI_CONSTITUTION.md) requires the represented business to remain the hero. Position JOHAI as the calm operating system that helps a business understand, decide, and serve—not as a novelty AI, generic chatbot, crowded dashboard, or autonomous replacement for people.

Sales claims must use simple human language, distinguish current capability from product direction, and never turn architecture principles into proof of deployment, outcomes, or traction. If a promise conflicts with the Ten Laws, Product Fact Sheet, security boundary, or evidence, it must not be made.

## Foundation positioning

Lead with the customer and business outcome defined by the [North Star](../foundation/JOHAI_NORTH_STAR.md) and [Mission and Vision](../foundation/MISSION_AND_VISION.md): clearer understanding, lower effort, accountable action, human authority, and protected data boundaries for owner-led businesses. The [Manifesto](../foundation/JOHAI_MANIFESTO.md) explains why this matters; the [Product Promise](../foundation/PRODUCT_PROMISE.md) defines the experience commitments that sales may describe.

Use the [Brand Guidelines](../foundation/BRAND_GUIDELINES.md) and [Voice and Tone](../foundation/VOICE_AND_TONE.md): simple, professional, calm, confident, human, respectful, and never arrogant, robotic, manipulative, or theatrical. Foundation language expresses JOHAI's adopted direction; current product claims must still be supported by the Product Fact Sheet and present evidence.

## Status discipline

See [Product Fact Sheet](../PRODUCT_FACT_SHEET.md) for Implemented, Partial, and Planned classifications. Update this chapter when the corresponding product behavior changes.

## Contextual Intelligence™ positioning

Position the philosophy as: **JOHAI reduces customer effort by using the authorized business and workflow context already available, then offering the smallest useful next step at the right time.** The intended feeling is “the business understands me,” not “I am talking to another AI bot.”

Do not claim that every described smart document, appointment, message, payment, support, order, reservation, invoice, or industry experience is live. Do not promise autonomous actions, perfect inference, regulated advice, human response times, provider synchronization, or measurable savings until the corresponding implementation and evidence exist.

Sales may describe [Contextual Intelligence](../philosophy/CONTEXTUAL_INTELLIGENCE.md) as the official product-design direction and explain its permission, silence, honesty, and human-escalation rules. Current capability statements must still come from the Product Fact Sheet.

## Customer Portal positioning

The repository implements a separate customer experience for provisioned users, powered discreetly by JOHAI and isolated from the business workspace. Sales must not present it as production-available or commercially validated until deployment configuration, customer provisioning, end-to-end tenant/storage testing, and customer validation are confirmed. Public signup and invitation redemption are not part of V1.

## Customer Portal Contextual Intelligence V1 positioning

Permitted positioning:

- JOHAI has implemented a bounded, typed Customer Portal context engine over already customer-visible records.
- The local development demo shows deterministic fictional dashboard ranking, appointment preparation, unsent message suggestions and rewrites, document guidance with source warnings, profile guidance, and one contextual support question.
- The design keeps the represented business primary, preserves customer confirmation, and fails closed when a source, permission, or approved provider is unavailable.

Required qualification:

- Dashboard, appointment, profile, and support record-based guidance is implemented.
- Document and message generation is **demo-only** through a deterministic fictional provider.
- The authenticated production generative provider, approved customer-visible knowledge publication, production document-content projection, and operational human handoff are unavailable or planned.
- Customer Portal remains 71/100, certification FAILED, and Production Ready: NO.

Do not claim autonomous action, live AI understanding, clinical or legal interpretation, invented appointment availability, human assignment, measured customer outcomes, a production moat, or production certification.
