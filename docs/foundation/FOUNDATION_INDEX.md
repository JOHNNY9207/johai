## Authority and interpretation

When documents appear to conflict, use this order:

1. law, binding obligations, security, privacy, accessibility, explicit human approval, and mandatory database safety;
2. the Constitution;
3. constitutional companion principles;
4. strategic identity, Contextual Intelligence, brand, culture, AI Employee, and roadmap documents;
5. architecture, policies, manuals, specifications, sprint records, and implementation.

The Foundation must be interpreted cumulatively. A lower document may be more specific or more protective but may not weaken a higher authority.

## Change control

This index and every document it identifies as part of the Foundation are locked at Version 1.0. Changes require an explicit user request and the [Foundation Change Policy](FOUNDATION_CHANGE_POLICY.md). No implementation may silently redefine a Foundation term or create an exception through code, configuration, copy, or local documentation.
